Code Red

A full day, story based event which test physical strengths and problem solving skills.

Story: The Governor is under custody of hijackers.If basic tests are qualified ,common people can join rescuing process. Huge reward is declared by the central government to those who rescue Governor.

Register

Team: 2-3 participants
Registration fee: 100 per person
Prize:
1 st Prize - Rs 7500
2 nd Prize - Rs 3500
3 rd Prize - Rs 2000
Duration: 48 hours

Rules and Regulations:
● Participants should qualify the dark-room test.
● Unique ID is given to qualified teams
● Different puzzles to reach secret vault is given(the questions are related to both technical and non-technical)
● Solve the puzzle and formulate the secret code
● Try to open lockers using formulated code, the key and location of secret vault is in these lockers
● Reach secret vault. Obstacles,explosives,bombs etc will be there ,the participants has to diffuse it using the wiring diagram
● Execute enemies around the Governor
● Evacuate Governor within the time limit
