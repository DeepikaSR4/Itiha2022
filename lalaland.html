Rules and Regulations:
● A band should have 3-10 participants.
● No individual can be part of more than one competing band in any scenario.
● Guide tracks, backing tracks, pre recorded music or computed beats are not allowed.
● Maximum of 20 min including sound check will be provided
● Exceeding the time limit given by organizers will leads to deduction of points
● Drum sets will be provided. Bands are requested to bring any other equipment if required.
● All music should be played live on stage.
● In case of any conflicts the decision of judges and coordinators will be final and binding.
