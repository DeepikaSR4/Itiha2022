ValRena


A Tournament style gaming competition where the winner takes all. Participants are required to bring their own devices.

Register

Team : 5 participants required
Registration fee: 300 per team
Prizes
1st prize : 3000
2nd prize : 1250
3rd prize : 500


Rules and regulation:
● All players must comply with the rules set by the tournament organizers. Failure to do so can result in disqualification.
● Participants must follow the tournament schedule determined by the tournament organizers.
● Participants are requested to check the ping and the system prior to the competition.
● Any player can register only once to play in ONE team.
● In case of System failure, Game will be restarted.
● All forms of cheating in matches are prohibited and will be penalized.
