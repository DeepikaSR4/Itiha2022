Rules and Regulations:
1. Own choice round:
● Participants can sing a song of their own choice for a time limit of 5 minutes.
● Two copies of karaoke should be brought by the participants in CD's or Pendrives.
2. Spot Round:
● Participants will be given a word on spot and are supposed to sing maximum number of songs containing this word within 5 minutes.
3. Judges Round:
● Participants are required to sing a song based on the theme given by the judges on the spot within 1 minute.
● The decisions of the judges and the coordinators will be final and binding in all cases.
● Minimum number of participants is 4.
● Valid college I'd should be presented.
● Should report at the location 30 minutes prior the event
