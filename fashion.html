Registration fee: 150 per head

Rules and Regulations:
● One entry per college.
● Theme selection is open. All the teams will have their own individual theme.
● A team can have up to 25 models excluding designers, choreographers and supporting members.
● Performance time would be a minimum of 6 minutes to a maximum of 15 minutes. All teams should spend the initial few minutes of their performance time for introduction.
● Negative marking if participants exceed the time limit.
● One person per team should be there to manage the sound tracks and one for the lights. No supporting staffs will be provided.
● Emphasis will be given to clothing,walking style, originality and attitude.
● No props and costumes will be provided.
● Use of materials like candles, matches,cigarettes, alcohol and any other hazardous material on the stage is prohibited.
● Two copies of sound track should be submitted in advance of standard audio format .wav or .mp3 of sound quality 192 kb/s or above to organisers (in CD's and pendrives, no laptops)
● Vulgarity of any kind would lead to disqualification of the team.
● The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
● It is imperative that all team members must possess a valid college ID which must be presented at the time of registration.
● Professional make- up artist and choreographer can be hired.
● A green room will be provided for dressing purpose.
● Special appreciation will be given for the best model male and female.
● Minimum participants 10.
● Should bring valid college id cards.
