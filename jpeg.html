Rules and Regulations:
● Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline lapses.
● Only Mobile photography is acceptable.
● Maximum 4 entries per participant.
● The judging panel shall assess and determine the winning Photos.
● Photographs that have been digitally altered beyond standard optimization (removal of dust, cropping, reasonable adjustments to exposure, color and contrast, etc.) will be disqualified.
● Entries will be judged equally on creativity, technical expertise and aesthetic value.
● If demanded/required, participants shall produce the original digital soft copy photo for verification.
