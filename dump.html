Dump and Dumper


Mimicry is the art of imitation, which is practiced through out the world. From annoying siblings who repeat every word you say, to professional comedians who impersonate famous people onstage.
Mahathma Gandhi once said “In a gentle way you can shake the world, for that gentle change, Make a smile on each face”.

Register

Registration fee: 150 per head

Rules and Regulations:
● This is a solo event.
● Each participants shall be given 5 minutes.
● Participants may mimic sounds of machines, birds, animals, gadgets, musical instruments and speeches of well known persons including film personalities.
● Judgement will be based on the imitating skills, variety of sounds and voices imitated
● Medium of expression will be Malayalam, Hindi or English.
● No vulgar or unparliamentary language will be entertained.
● It should necessarily be cultured and pleasant piece of art.
● The decision made by judge will be final.
