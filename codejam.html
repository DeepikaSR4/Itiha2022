Code Jam

A one day coding competition for participants to hone their coding skills with algorithmic puzzles meant for students.

Register

Individual
Registration fee: 150 per person
Prize: 3000
Duration: 1 day event

Rules and regulations:
● Allowed programming languages are C, C++, Java, and Python.
● All questions have a certain time limit depending on the difficulty.
● No external electronic data may be accessed at any time. This includes phones, USB drives.
● If a question is unclear, participants may request clarification from the judges.
● Looking at others' work/solutions, using disallowed external resources, or attempting to hack the contest environment will result in disqualification.
● Any form of cheating or misconduct will result in direct disqualification.
● Candidates will be judged on the basis of accuracy of the programs and its output as well as the time taken.
● The winner is decided on the basis of the number of problems solved.
● Ties will be broken by the total time of the user in ascending order.
